## Pair plot 

Diagonal entries show estimates of the marginal 
densities as well as the (0.16, 0.5, 0.84) 
quantiles (dotted lines). 
Off-diagonal entries show estimates of the pairwise 
densities. 

Movie linked below (🍿) superimposes 
100 iterations 
of MCMC.
